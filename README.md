📊 ApexMart Sales Performance Dashboard
---
📌 Project Overview

The **ApexMart Sales Performance Dashboard** is an end-to-end data analytics project built using Power BI.
The dashboard analyzes retail sales data (2020–2021) to deliver clear insights into revenue performance, profitability, category trends, and geographic distribution.
Designed for executive-level reporting and data-driven decision-making.

---

 🎯 Business Objective

* Monitor overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top and underperforming product categories
* Analyze monthly sales trends
* Understand city-level revenue contribution
* Evaluate customer payment preferences

---

🧠 Data Modeling Approach

* Implemented a **Star Schema** data model
* Created separate Fact and Dimension tables
* Built a dedicated Date table for time intelligence
* Established optimized relationships for performance

---

📊 Key KPIs Tracked

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity
* Average Order Value
* Sales Growth %

---

📈 Dashboard Highlights

* Sales & Profit Trend Analysis
* Category-wise Sales & Profit Comparison
* City-wise Sales Distribution (Map)
* Payment Mode Analysis
* Interactive Filters (Year, Month, Category, City)

---

💡 Key Insights

* Strong sales growth observed from 2020 to 2021
* Grocery category drives the highest revenue
* Electronics shows higher profit margins
* Digital payments dominate transaction volume
* Seasonal peak visible in Q4

---

🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Power Query
* Data Modeling (Star Schema)
